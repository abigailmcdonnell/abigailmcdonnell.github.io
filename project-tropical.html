Georgia Tech REU · 2023 · Advisor: Prof. Jie He

Tropical Precipitation Projections

Overview

As an REU student at Georgia Tech under Prof. Jie He, studied tropical precipitation projections in relation to present-day sea surface temperatures. Tested a perturbed physics ensemble to identify parameters most responsible for influencing tropical precipitation projections and analyzed the relationship between model parameters and tropical precipitation sensitivity.
