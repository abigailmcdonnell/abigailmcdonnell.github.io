About Me

I am a first-year graduate student at the University of Miami studying under Dr. Brian Soden. I’m interested in how physical processes influence model projections and the accuracy of climate predictions. My research focuses on analyzing climate models computationally to better understand how to predict a changing climate.

My work combines satellite observations, model diagnostics, and statistical analyses to diagnose and reduce biases in climate projections.
